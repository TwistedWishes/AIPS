Our History

The American Institute of Psychical Studies was founded in 1966 by pharmaceutical entrepreneur Edward Harris. Harris is known for the creation and popularization of the G-11 auditory treatment, which is now authorized for administration in over 200 American hospitals and medical facilities. For over six decades, AIPS has strived to research and examine the human brain's internal processes in order to better everyday life for everday people.

Our Staff

AIPS employs only the best and brightest minds in the field of biological research for our studies. Through our partnerships with many top American universities and scientific education programs, AIPS can source, train, and recruit new researchers for our ever-expanding slate of projects and proposals in order to maximize our impact in the greater scientific community.

Our Values

The American Institute of Psychical Studies has always valued effort, dedication, and most importantly, knowledge. We believe that cultivating new psychical discoveries and sharing them is our best path forward as an organization, as a nation, and as a society. AIPS is universally committed to the pursuit of a better tomorrow through the continued study of the human psyche.
